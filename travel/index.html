Travel

Backpacking, skiing and nature photography

I write Kotlin for a living. The rest of the time I'm out with a pack on — trails, ski slopes, and a plan to be somewhere new by morning. Mostly the North Atlantic, as it turns out: cliffs, waterfalls, and weather that ruins half the plans. Some of it shot from the ground and some from a drone, depending on what the day allows. Home base is Tirana. Tap anything to open it.
